Formulas & assumptions

Key formulas used by this demo for quick estimates.

MetricFormula
Pesticide avoided (lbs/year)acres × baseline_lbs_per_acre × SIT_reduction%
GHG avoided (t CO₂e)pesticide_avoided_lbs × 5 kgCO₂e/lb ÷ 1000
Water saved (gal)acres × 3 cycles × 200 gal
Food saved (tons)production_tons × baseline_loss% × irradiation_shelf_improvement%

These are simplified heuristics for demo purposes. Replace constants with validated domain data for production.
